High School Physical Science Reference Sheet

Equations:

  • Change of x equals x final minus x initial
  • Average velocity equals Change in x over change of time equals final x minus initial x over final time over initial time.
  • Average acceleration equals change in velocity over change in time equals final velocity minus initial velocity over final time over initial time.
  • Net force equals mass times acceleration
  • Gravitational force equals mass times gravitational field strength
  • Kinetic energy equals one half mass times velocity squared
  • Gravitational potential energy equal mass times gravitational field strength times height
  • Work equals Force times change in x

Constant:

  • Gravitational field strength equals ten meters per second squared equals ten newtons per kilograms

Variables and Symbols:

  • Δ = change in a value (final - initial)
  • a = acceleration
  • aavg = average acceleration
  • Eg = gravitational potential energy
  • Ek = kinetic energy
  • Fnet = net force
  • Fg = gravitational force
  • g = gravitational field strength
  • h = height
  • m = mass
  • t = time
  • v = speed
  • v = velocity
  • vavg = average velocity
  • W = work
  • x = position
