Question Number 2

The following question has two parts. First, answer part A. Then, answer part B.

Part A

Choose the sentence that best explains what the use of dialogue shows about the relationship between Sam and his older brother, Tim.

 

It shows that they care about each other.

 

It shows that Tim does not want Sam to be lazy.

 

It shows that Tim needs Sam's help to finish the painting.

 

It shows that Tim tries to make Sam do things he does not like doing.

Part B

Which sentences from the passage best support your answer in Part A?

 

“‘Sam, you can't just sit there.’”

 

“On the other hand, Tim, Sam's older brother, made painting seem easy.”

 

“Sam was not sure what his role would be.”

 

“‘I don't want to ruin any of your ideas.’”
